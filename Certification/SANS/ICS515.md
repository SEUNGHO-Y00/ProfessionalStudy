### The ICS Information Attack Space

* Threat Pool (Continued Growth in Two Dimensions)
  - IT/OT Convergence
  - IT/OT convergence connects physical industrial machines to digital business networks. It merges OT (Operational Technology, which controls physical equipment like factory robots) with IT (Information Technology, which handles data like emails and databases). This allows companies to use real-time data to stop machine breakdowns before they happen.
  - Breaking Down the Two Worlds
    - OT: The brawn. These are sensors, valves, and motors running a factory floor. They use older, specialized languages.
    - IT: The brains. This is the cloud storage, business software, and cybersecurity networks. It uses standard internet language.
